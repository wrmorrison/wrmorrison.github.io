"In order to imprint process, I would like to have the opportunity to do an exercise ""from scratch"" on my own computer with the instructor present.  I recognize that this might not be practical, unless it were a course requirement for students to bring their own laptops to class.

Another general comment relevant to computer workshops in general--in order to ""imprint learning,"" provide verbal cues and ask students to repeat orally.  e.g.: ""Repeat after me: I have just created a mask based on a selection...."" Then ask students to describe physical steps on keyboard... (may sound retarded, but my contention is that learning computer skills is much like learning a musical instrument--so it's language/rhythm patterns must be understood and absorbed through repetition.)"
